As per usual, please make sure that the uploaded CSVs maintain the existing requirements. I've listed them all out here, for your convenience:

1. The CSV must have at least 6 columns: account_number, debtor_name, phone_number, balance, status, client_name
2. Each row in the CSV must have an entry for these 6 columns
3. The CSV must not have duplicate account_numbers
4. The 'balance' column must be a numerical value and free of symbols (e.g. $ is not allowed)
5. The 'status' column must be either "Active" or "Closed"

If you upload another CSV that has a row with the same account number in the database, that account number's information will be updated. That way, you can continue to update your spreadsheet and easily sync it with our system.
